In Shattered Bridge, one of the secret areas requires the use of a
unisolid pipe top.  Since making all pipe tops unisolid causes
problems, I've added another tile for the cases where a unisolid pipe
is needed.  I've also added a "Unisolid" tilegroup because the second
pipe top might confuse level designers if it was in the "Pipe"
tilegroup.
