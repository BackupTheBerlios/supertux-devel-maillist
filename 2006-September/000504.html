I have an idea for creating frozen images of bagdguys in Gimp:
1) Make a new layer as wide as the image, but only 1 pixel high (2 or 3
works fine).
2) Render a solid-noise cloud with Filters > Render > Clouds > Solid
Noise...
3) Scale the layer to the full image size.
4) Make the layer between 50 and 70% opaque.
5) Go to Layer > Colors > Colorize, and crank up the lightness (about 25 to
50%), and move the hue to between 180 and 200 degrees.
6) Go to the badguy's layer, hit Z, and select the transparency, then go
back and trim the frost layer.
I'm doing this to a series of badguys, and I'll upload the results when I'm
done.
