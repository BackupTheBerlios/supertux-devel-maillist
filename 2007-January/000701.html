I've written another patch which fixes some display bugs. The main menu is 
centered vertically, and the camera centers the level, if it is smaller, than 
the screen. Worldmap gets a background-color if necessary, and is centered 
too.
http://www.unix-ag.uni-kl.de/~kldenker/supertux/widescreen_fixes.patch

For testing, i included another command-line-switch, to scale the whole 
screen:
http://www.unix-ag.uni-kl.de/~kldenker/supertux/scale.patch

We should think about limiting the Values for the aspect ratio and the scale 
factor, to values of real screens. The aspect may be limited between 16:9 and 
5:4. (are 1280x1024 displays sized 5:4 or don't the have quadratic pixels?) 
The scale factor could be limited between 1.0 and 2.0.

Next thing, I'm going to do, is fix is the demo-recording. A demo recorded 
with different SCREEN_WIDTH or SCREEN_HEIGHT does not play correctly. The 
monsters don't spawn at the same points. So it's necessary to store these two 
values and change them for playback. The screen-resolution needs to be 
corrected too.
