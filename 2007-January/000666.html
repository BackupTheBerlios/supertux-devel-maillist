I alread mentioned it on IRC, but here again for the rest of you:

Is there any reason why Debian or any other distri for that matter,
should ship 0.3.0 in the first place? As I understand it, it was never
meant to be a 'real' release, instead it was meant as an
engine-preview demo kind of thing. Its full of bugs and annoyances,
the whole Milestone1 world got butchered into a mess, the difficulty
is out of balance and it requires OpenGL for no good reason. In short,
its *not* Milestone2 and its not something I would want to ship to a
wide audience.

So can't we just suggest to Debian and all the other distros out there
to continue shipping whatever is the latest release of Milestone1?
