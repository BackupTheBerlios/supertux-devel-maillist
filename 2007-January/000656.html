We should wait for Christoph to chime in; he's a SuperTux dev and he's

Additionally: no, I'm pretty sure performance will suck more with Mesa
than with the NoGL patch. The SDL-only version does everything in 2D,
keeping the performance around ST 0.1.x levels. Mesa -- believe me, I
tried back then on Linux with 2.4GHz -- makes the whole thing a slide
show, since it's doing 3D stuff for a 2D game on a CPU. Remember the
README: OpenGL and Mesa? 10GHz processor, please. Or buy yourself a
GeForce FX5200 or 6200 for a bargain bin price.
