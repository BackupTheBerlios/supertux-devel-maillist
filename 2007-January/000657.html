The 10GHz are basically something of a bitter joke. A normal CPU
probably won't be able to accelerate SuperTux with OpenGL, so I (I think
it was me) put this massive number in there. It means, in short, "Maybe
CPUs will sometime be able to accelerate SuperTux, but until then, just
buy yourself some outdated graphics card. It'll save you lots of pain."
