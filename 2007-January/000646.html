Hi I've made a few comments on the bug list about issues I had with sound 
effects being played
only on one channel, I'd prefer to have a stereo option as I am not 
convinced the sounds indicate
direction of origin sufficiently well..

Also I have problems with the separation of the "up" and "jump" controls as 
I am no longer able to
use the <up arrow> key to jump ingame and move up on the map - pressing up 
on the map enters a level...

The other issus I've come across are that some enemies display inconsistency 
when coming to the edge of platforms, sometimes they turn sometimes they 
fall - i've seen an ice-block fall off one platform, walk a bit and then 
turn at the next edge - doesn't make sense to me?

Other than that I find the shooting bonus a lot easier and fun to use - 
making it a much more sought-after bonus, and the bouncing snowballs are a 
lot less nasty in their following pattern than in version 0.1.3..

I'm not a fan of the way the screen scrolls back when you stop moving, but 
hopefully this will be fixed or tweaked in future

great work so far!
