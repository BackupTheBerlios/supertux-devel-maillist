Hello Supertux Developers!

I have made two patches for Supertux (SVN).
The Patches can be found at http://www.unix-ag.uni-kl.de/~kldenker/supertux/

The little lightmap_resolution.patch makes the Level "light+magic" playable at 
any screen resolution. It fixes a TODO to calculate the correct 
lightmap-coordinates in the function get_light.

The second patch is a Widescreen-Patch. It includes a new 
command-line-parameter "--aspect" or "-a". It defaults to 4:3 but can be 
changed to any other ratio. SCREEN_WIDTH and SCREEN_HEIGHT are calculated 
from these aspect-values. This caused some minor Problems with the 
message-boxes and the credits. But I fixed them already.

I hope you can use these patches,
Klaus Denker
