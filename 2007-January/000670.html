Beg pardon, but Linux and MIDI? I tried to create that relationship a 
few years back. Didn't work out. I'm pretty sure Wansti can acknowledge 
how hard it is to get it working. And yes, the Roland DLS sound bank 
distributed with Windows (and I fear Mac too) makes all the instruments 
sound like... it sucks so much I can't come up with a good analogy.

As for bruting the music down, "-q -1" of oggenc makes me hear a 
difference between the high-frequency abrupt sounds (= the claps in 
bossattack.ogg; the whole drum set in cave.ogg, chipdisko.ogg, 
forest3.ogg, fortress.ogg and theme.ogg; the flutes in forest.ogg and 
forest2.ogg) and I get ugly aural artifacts during the longer chords in 
leveldone*.ogg.

As for the sizes:
