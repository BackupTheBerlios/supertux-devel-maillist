On Thu, Jul 06, 2006 at 01:23:52PM +0200, Wolfgang Becker wrote:
> do we want leveldesigner to be able to change the sprites used for
> badguys? 

Short answer: No

Long answer: Consistency is important so we shouldn't break it by
allowing to much customization, however in some levels it might
make sense to allow customization (say, something like in the XMas
edition of Lemmings). Those stuff should however shouldn't be 
something ever seen in a normal level, but bonus/extra/special 
level stuff only.
