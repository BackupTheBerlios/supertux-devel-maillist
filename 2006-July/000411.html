Julian,

There is a game similar to what you describe except that it doesn't have 
Tux in it, just Mario-related characters.  It's called "Super Mario War" 
and can be found at:
http://freshmeat.net/projects/smw/
"Super Mario War is a Super Mario deathmatch game. The goal is to stomp 
as many other Marios as possible. It is a tribute to Mario War by 
Samuele Poletti."

It shouldn't be too hard to take some SuperTux sprites and replace an 
existing Mario character's sprites with it to get the effect you're 
looking for.  Alternatively, you could poke at their source-code and add 
Tux as a new character.  Also note that I've never actually played this 
game so I don't know if there are any other characters besides Mario.
