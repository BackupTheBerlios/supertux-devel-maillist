I don't do much of either code of graphics, but the single-image plus
arms for carrying something seems like a good solution to me. That way
it's easy to make Tux hold lots of different objects and also decide
where to hold them - for slipping through a one-tile-hole, it should be
easy to make his arms point upwards and hold the object over his head...
for example.
