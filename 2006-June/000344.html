I think I'm responsible for a bit of confusion. :-)
Rebecca, if you played only milestone 1 and didn't see a yeti, that's
okay because there wasn't one. You didn't miss anything. :-) However,
there was a level called "No more Mr Ice Guy" placed a few levels before
the castle. This level was supposed to contain a mini boss (hence the
title), while the final boss (the yeti) should be in the castle.
Unfortunately, neither of the bosses made it into the first milestone.
For milestone 2, we changed our plans a little: We dropped the mini
boss, and we placed the yeti in an extra level outside the castle. So
for milestone 2, I renamed the old "Ice Guy" level to "A Mysterious
House of Ice" and used the name "No more Mr Ice Guy" for the final boss
level.
